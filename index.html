Retrofit-ready sauna control kit

Bring a smarter, warmer ritual to any sauna.

Smart Sauna is a university-built hardware kit designed to modernize new or existing saunas without turning the whole room into a renovation project. It adds a 7-inch touchscreen, live environment sensing, session profiles, Bluetooth audio, LED mood control, and smoke detection in one clear package.

Why it matters

Built as a kit, so the upgrade feels practical.

Smart Sauna is meant to fit real buying decisions. Because it comes as a hardware kit, it can be integrated into a fresh sauna build or added to an existing sauna without redesigning the whole system. That makes it easier to install, easier to pitch, and easier to imagine in real use.

Customers can install it themselves, or choose a paid installation service if they want a more turnkey experience.

What it does

A calmer control experience around the sauna session.

The product is focused on visibility, atmosphere, safety, and repeatability. It is not trying to be everything at once.

01

Environment monitoring

Tracks temperature and humidity in real time so users can understand the sauna environment throughout a session instead of relying only on feel.

02

Custom session profiles

Save preferred session setups, then start and end them whenever you want. The goal is to make repeated rituals easy, personal, and quick to access.

03

LED and audio atmosphere

Control LED colors and play music through the sauna's Bluetooth speakers, either from the touchscreen itself or directly from a connected phone.

04

Smoke detection

Additional smoke sensing improves awareness and adds a practical safety layer to the overall kit.

05

Expandable platform

Future versions are planned to support interaction with ESPHome over MQTT for deeper smart-home style automation.

University project

Designed as a serious product direction, not only a class demo.

Smart Sauna is being developed by a university team as a real product concept with practical positioning. The aim is to explore a solution that can become commercially interesting, not just technically possible.

That is why the page focuses on who it helps, how it fits into existing saunas, and why the kit approach matters.
